Our Story

Fifteen Years of Literary Excellence

Founded in 2010 by a team of bestselling authors and veteran editors, InkCraft began as a boutique studio with a single mission: to give every great story the professional voice it deserves.

Today, we're an agency of over 120 elite ghostwriters, developmental editors, and publishing consultants spanning 18 countries — having helped deliver 24,000+ books that have collectively reached millions of readers worldwide.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Excellence Without Compromise

Every manuscript we produce undergoes a rigorous multi-stage review. We never submit work below the highest literary and professional standard.

Absolute Confidentiality

Your story is your intellectual property — always. Every client and project is protected by iron-clad NDAs and strict internal privacy protocols.

Collaborative Partnership

We listen first. Your voice, vision, and goals drive every decision. We're your partner, not just a vendor — invested in your success at every milestone.
