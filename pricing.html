Project timelines vary by scope and complexity. A short book (15,000–25,000 words) typically takes 6–10 weeks. A full novel or memoir (60,000–80,000 words) usually runs 4–6 months. We provide a detailed project timeline before we begin and stick to it with milestone tracking.
We write across 15+ genres including fiction (fantasy, thriller, romance, sci-fi, literary), non-fiction (business, memoir, self-help, health, Spiritual), children's books, and academic works. Each project is matched to a writer with specific genre expertise.
All Professional and Elite plan clients receive unlimited revision rounds at no additional cost. We also offer a money-back guarantee on the first chapter or sample — if you're not satisfied after the initial sample, we'll fully refund your deposit, no questions asked.
We begin with a 1–2 hour discovery call where we learn about your project goals, audience, tone, and vision. We may ask for reference materials, existing notes, outlines, or voice samples. Our project managers guide you through our complete onboarding questionnaire to capture everything needed.
